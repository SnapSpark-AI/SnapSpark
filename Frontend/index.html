This is SnapSpark.


SnapSpark is an AI-powered, crowdsourced wildfire prediction platform. Relying on users to upload images of their surroundings, our machine learning model provides real-time predictions to enable rapid response to fire threats. We aim to empower communities to take immediate action towards wildfire prediction and prevention, one snap at a time.
